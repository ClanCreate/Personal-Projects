Kuudra

The Art of T5 Kuudra

Kuudra is a 4-player instance accessible in the Crimson Isles. Players unlock this instance by doing the main quest given by Elle when they first enter the Crimson Isles. There are 5 tiers of Kuudra that players may unlock by gaining the sufficiant reputation in their faction. This guide will focus primarily on the fifth and last tier of Kuudra. The techniques and know how mentioned in this guide may be utilized in lower tiers of Kuudra.

Phase 1: Supplies

The first part of the Kuudra instance is the supplies phase. Players must gather the 6 crates spawned in 7 different areas and bring them to the center in order to initiate the build phase. The meta for this phase is for each player to start in one of 4 crate spawning areas. These areas are represented by the symbol on the ground near them or structures. e.g. X, /, Tri, =, shop, x-cannon. When the run starts, each player will use their Open Shop to purchase the Specialist class and pre-throw their rod to speed up the crate gathering process.

Phase 2: Supplies Part 2

Once successful in obtaining the crate, players will place their crate in the pile closest to their respective starting spot. After players obtain their first crate, they do their following 2nd crate which is determined by which of the crate didnt spawn that run. This is easily noted due to needing to collect 6 crates and only having 7 spawning locations. This way, everyone would follow an order determined by which crate didn't spawn.

Phase 3: Building The Ballista

After placing all 6 crates, players should head to the closest build pile respective to their pre spot location. Then they quickly clear mobs to gain tokens and purchase the highest lvl of build mechanic possible. Should they be unable to purchase Build Mechanic II, players should build the ballista until they obtain the tokens to purchase the upgrade. The only caveat is if you have Fresh. Players with Fresh buff should continue to build for the rest of the phase as it's considered not worth the time loss on Fresh to purchase an upgrade to Build Mechanic. Players should stand 1 block diagonal of the center of the crate pile. Doing so allows the player to look straight down and hype and fire veil whilst still shift and left clicking to continue building. Once their first pile is complete, the player should hype to the next pile on their half of the build and continue. Should your side be complete before the other side, clear mobs attacking your build and go help the other side of the build.

Stunner Role

Stun should leave build ~92% and proceed to the left cannon facing Kuudra. This is due to the cannon animation being slightly faster. Once the stunner is next to the cannon, they should spam buy cannonball in the Open Shop until build is done which will enable them to purchase cannonball. Once purchased, the stunner should instantly exit Open Shop and left click the cannon and aim at kuudra. Once inside kuudra, the stunner should activate etherwarp conduit to a designated spot and use the hotm 3 perk pickobulus to break the kuudra cell. Once done, stunner should pearl to a designed spot in Kuudra to animation skip the death animation and get teleported down to the Damage Phase.

Phase 4: Stun Phase

Once build is complete, all players but stunner will go over to the right side facing kuudra and start shooting with at least 32 toxic arrow poison in their inventory. Toxic arrow poison (tap) triggers every tick on kuudra and when kuudra, is stunned, also gets the buffed damage which allows for a fast stun phase.

Damage buffs

Before entering Damage Phase, there are various damage buffs that players may utilize to speed up the last round. The most common one is lifeline. Lifeline is an attribute obtained from killing the chicken jockey in the crimson isles. The buff gives 25% multiplicative damage which drastically improves the parties dps. In order to activate the buff, players must be less than 20% hp. To lose hp and stay below 20% hp, player utilize various items and an equip called lava shell necklace; prevents all forms of hp regen. Other forms of damage buffs;
weirder tuba: buffs all party members by
Sword of bad health: +100 str for 5s
SoS flare: +10 ferocity and jalapeno buffs

Phase 5: Damage Phase

Once kuudra is stunned and defeated, t5 kuudra's unique phase starts. All players should rag + reaper instantly after stun phase so they get full buffs when they are teleported down and should instantly start shooting kuudra till it is defeated.
